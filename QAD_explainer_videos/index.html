Phase 1

ZZ-Kernel Prescreen

Hadamard + RZ(2x_i) + CNOT·RZ(2x_ix_j)·CNOT builds a quantum-kernel similarity to a reference set. The top-k% pool advances.
S_K = 1 − ⟨φ(x), φ(x')⟩_R
 Phase 2

Quantum Autoencoder

Hardware-efficient ansatz compresses 8 qubits → 3 latent qubits; SWAP-test on trash qubits measures fidelity; 1−F is the anomaly score.
S_A = 1 − F(trash, |0⟩⁵)
 Phase 3

QAOA Marginal Selector

Variational circuit on the Phase 2 pool: U_C(γ) and U_B(β) alternating layers pick the final candidates (number is tuner output per dataset) by minimising an Ising Hamiltonian.
H_C = α·Σsᵢ Zᵢ + (1−α)·Σdᵢⱼ ZᵢZⱼ
